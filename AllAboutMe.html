All About Me!

It's Just Me!

My name is Wiltiana but everyone calls me Tiana. I am in the 12th grade and I go to Charles Drew High School. My favorite subuject is Math. Here you will learn what I do and how I do it. You will collect good tips on how to be a leader and create your own wave.

This is me. I always have the biggest smile on my face and I am very goofy. However, there is a serious side to my goofiness. I have 3 different jobs and I love all 3 of them. First, I am a Volunteer Coordinator for The Smith Group and the NAACP Clayton County Branch, Second, I am Secretary for the NAACP Clayton County Branch Youth Council, and Third, I am a former Girls Who Code participate.
